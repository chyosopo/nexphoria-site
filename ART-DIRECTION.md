# NEXPHORIA — OUTCOME IMAGERY LAW (C29)
*Every image is a hyper-real lifestyle photograph of the OUTCOME the protocol buys — the moment after it worked. Never product-first, never abstract. The viewer must feel "that's the me I'm buying."*

## GLOBAL GRADE (locked once test pair approved)
- Hyper-realistic editorial photography: 85mm look, shallow depth, natural directional light, true skin texture (pores, fine lines kept — aspiration, not plastic)
- Casting: believable 32–52, athletic-normal not fitness-model; diverse across the set
- Palette lives in the ENVIRONMENT, not a filter: men = cool porcelain light, slate/navy wardrobe & surfaces; women = warm blush light, rose/cream environments
- No text, no logos, no vials in outcome shots (vials live only in spec/clinical slots)
- Emotion is carried by BODY LANGUAGE + LIGHT, one clear story per frame
