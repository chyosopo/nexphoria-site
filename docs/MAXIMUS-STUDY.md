## 2. The genius: the protocol-selector offer
Their /testosterone page is not one product — it's a **decision surface**. Six
protocols rendered as comparison cards, each with the same skeleton:

| Element | Example |
|---|---|
| Name | "Topical Testosterone + Enclomiphene" |
| Badges | *Patented Formulation · Fertility-Friendly* |
| **Best for:** persona line | "Men who want an easy once-daily topical while maintaining fertility markers." |
| 3 benefit bullets | "Combines natural production with topical T · higher levels than Enclomiphene alone · protects fertility" |
| Price anchor | "Starting at $189.99" |
| Free cross-sell | "Add Tadalafil to your protocol for **free**. Learn more →" |

The six: Enclomiphene ($99), Oral T ($149), Topical T ($109), Oral+Enclomiphene
($199), Topical+Enclomiphene ($189), Injectable TRT, Injectable+hCG ($299).
Segmented on three axes at once: **goal × fertility × format** (pill / cream /
injection). The customer self-selects; a doctor confirms. That's the model.

> **For Nexphoria:** we already have stacks + solos, but they're a flat shelf.
> The lift is to turn each GOAL page into a **protocol-selector** — 2–4 routes
> to the same goal, each a "Best for: [persona]" card with a price anchor, so
> the visitor sees themselves and picks. This is the single biggest structural
> idea to steal.

## 3. Goal-selector quizzes (interactive entry)
Embedded on the page, not a separate funnel: **"Why do you want to boost
testosterone?"** with clickable answers (Sharper focus & drive · Increased
libido · Elevated energy & mood), and **"What's your weight loss goal?"** (Lose
the last 5–10 lbs · Eat less, manage cravings). Each selection routes to the
matching protocol. Low-commitment, personalized, on-page.

> **For Nexphoria:** our goal tiles are static links. Make the home an
> interactive **"What do you want to change?"** selector that routes to a
> pre-filtered protocol view — the felt-need entry we've been circling.

Testosterone Calculator, BMI Calculator, TDEE Calculator, **Peptide Dosage

> **For Nexphoria:** build 2–3 real calculators (a Biological-Age estimator, a

## 5. Offer & pricing mechanics
- **"50% OFF YOUR FIRST MONTH FOR NEW CUSTOMERS PURCHASING 12-MONTH PLAN"** —
  annual commitment gated behind a first-month discount. (We adopt the
  commitment ladder; the discount energy needs Chiya's voice call — likely a
  softer "your first panel included" rather than "50% OFF".)
- **Add-on / upsell:** "Building Blocks" prescription multivitamins — "power up
  your testosterone treatment." A margin-accretive attach.
- **Free cross-sell:** "Add Tadalafil for free" — raises perceived value,
  increases stickiness.
- **Compounded vs brand-name tiers** at different prices (choice architecture).

> **For Nexphoria:** add a supporting-supplement/add-on tier and a genuinely
> free cross-sell (e.g., the bloodwork panel framed as included), and surface
> the annual commitment as a first-class selector (we started this with the
> cadence billing note).

## 6. Journey (3 steps, repeated)
Complete your evaluation (intake + at-home lab test — *"No commitment to buy"*)
→ Get your prescription (licensed doctor finalizes from labs + goals) →
Optimize your results (monitor, adjust dose with your doctor). Plus 24/7 doctor
messaging and a Discord community.

## 7. Proof
"Real clients. Real results." testimonials tagged with the protocol ("Using:
Enclomiphene"); a hard clinical stat — **"mean 2–3× increase in Total
Testosterone"** / "↑3.1x Free testosterone" — always with "Individual results
vary"; a growing Discord community; whitepapers and clinical studies.

> **For Nexphoria:** the numeric proof + protocol-tagged testimonials are the
> gap (blocked on Chiya for real testimonials). The research/whitepaper library
> is a trust moat we can build with true, cited content.

A large **/resources** library (30+ educational articles: "Free vs Total

section with whitepapers, a blog, and a community. This is a programmatic
organic-acquisition machine.

## 9. What to reject / adapt (Nexphoria law)
- Aggressive **"50% OFF"** discount energy → institutional-bank voice; reframe
  as value-included, not markdown.
- Synthetic-looking testimonials / unverifiable averages → true, consented
  proof only.
- Their look is fine but generic; Nexphoria's Porcelain & Navy + two-worlds is
  a stronger, more ownable identity — keep ours.
- Two-worlds: Maximus is men-only. Our her/him split is a differentiator to
  lean into, not flatten.
